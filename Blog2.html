Who Owns What You Consume?

Image from https://pixabay.com/photos/remote-control-tv-watch-tv-watch-4891936/ Who own's 7 News and Who are they? 7 News is owned and operated by Kerry Stokes in the chairman of the seven media group which also involves 7, Yahoo, Pacific Magazines and many more (Kerry Stokes). Kerry Stokes has held his position since 1999 and before that he was very involved through management of the group (Kerry Stokes). Him and a few others hold nearly 50% of the company shares for their own. Why does it matter that they own 7 News? There are a few reasons why the owner of the 7 News is significantly important to us viewers. Most people don't think about how the news can be modified or who the owners can impact the company significantly. According to an academic study, the main owners shape the news stories to serve their own political views to try and convince their audience (Johan 2019). The paper also states big companies shifting and controlling the news outlets to the public (Johan 2019). These issues become a worry for audiences as their opinion can be shifted without them even knowing (Johan 2019). Which is why viewers should be careful what they consume. What Trust do you have in 7 News? Trust in big news companies like 7 news rely on the viewer. The positive of trusting these companies is that you get the stories of what is happening within the country while also around the world. Stated above, these companies can bring negatives towards the viewers. Trusting can also lead to shifts with views especially through election times and the lack of public voices especially local news.
